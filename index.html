Getting started

1️⃣Find the bot on Telegram → send /start
2️⃣Run /calculate to set your protein & calorie targets
3️⃣Start logging meals — by photo or by typing
4️⃣Come back here with your Telegram ID to view your data

Logging meals

📷Send any food photo — hawker food, home meals, packaged items
⌨️Or just type what you ate — no photo needed:

nasi lemak
chicken breast 180g raw
fish, tofu, kangkung, rice ← multiple dishes at once

Useful commands

📊/summary — today's protein & calorie totals
✏️/edit — edit or delete today's meals
⚙️/setup — preferences, tone, bloating toggle
❓/help — interactive help, or just ask "how do I..."

Tips

💡Add a caption to photos for better accuracy
💡Estimates for Malaysian dishes may vary ±20–30%
💡Run /calculate every 6–8 weeks as your body changes
